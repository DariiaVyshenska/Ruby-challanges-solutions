Data Definition Language (DDL) - language used to define and manipulate the
schema (the structure of the database, relations and tables within it).
Examples: CREATE, DROP, ALTER
Data Manipulation Language (DML) - language used to retrieve and modify the data
stored within a database.
Examples: SELECT, UPDATE, INSERT, DELETE
